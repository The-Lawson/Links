Parametrically Designed Figures

Rhinocerous 3D, Grasshopper, 2023

The project was involved in a course focused on parametric designs, in which a programmed sequence would generate randomized objects with various patterns. These figures were initially created from a trapezoid being randomly rotated at a randomly generated axel (Slide 1). The new shape was then extruded with other shapes at different generated angles to create new complex forms (Slide 2). The patterns were created in a similar way, with an initial pattern being set up with randomly generated distances between lines and line segments (Slide 4). From here, the patterns were projected onto the forms (Slide 5) and the new forms were then projected onto a 2D space to create complex works of art (Slides 7 and 8).
