My Artworks

Growing up, my house always had a cabinet filled with art supplies varying from paint to countless Crayola markers. I have been drawing terribly ever since I was little, and drawing well ever since I started taking classes in middle school. I have always enjoyed making art and exploring new mediums. I always keep a sketchbook in my bag just in case I come across something that inspires me.
